History

Through the course of history, Chinese Characters had six major evolution. Chinese characters have evolved through six important periods. Each period of script is different from others. Chinese characters have evolved from “oracle bone script” to “bronze script” to “small seal script” to “clerical script” to “regular script” to modern simplified Chinese characters.
